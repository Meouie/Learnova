About us

At Learnova, we believe education should inspire, not overwhelm. With rigid curricula and rising student stress, today’s system often leaves learners disengaged and unmotivated. We set out to change that by blending the joy of gaming with the power of learning.

Our platform transforms study material into interactive challenges, rewards consistency, and makes knowledge fun to explore. As SIH 2024 Finalists, our team is driven to create a future where students don’t just prepare for exams — they enjoy the process of learning and grow with it.
